# Zeke's Bar Mitzvah

<p style="font-size: 1.1em; color: #555; margin-top: -0.5em;">Saturday, May 9, 2026. A Torah service in the redwoods, a party in Piedmont, and an MCP server because nerds.</p>

Hi! If you're a human, this page has everything you need for the weekend. If you're an AI agent, scroll to the bottom and have a field day. Either way, welcome.

**Cell reception may be limited in the redwoods.** You probably want to download, print, or memorize this page.

## Morning Torah Service

Saturday May 9, 2026, 10:30am – 12:45pm.

- **Location:** Redwood Glen Picnic Area, a short walk from the Redwood Glen Trailhead.
- **Time:** The service starts at 10:30am, but we recommend arriving by 10am.
- **Attire:** "Redwood Torah casual." A button-up shirt and sneakers is perfect.

### Getting there

1. Parking at the trailhead is limited. Plan to park in Roberts Regional, just down the road from Redwood Glen. Parking is $5.
2. Walk from the parking lot:
   1. Walk back out of the Roberts Regional parking lot.
   2. Turn right onto Skyline Blvd for 500 feet.
   3. Cross the street and follow the trail to the picnic site.

**Pro tip:** drop your family at the Trailhead, then send one driver back to park.

If you get lost, look for ~200 Jews wandering in the forest.

Saturday May 9, 2026, 6:00pm – 10:00pm.
