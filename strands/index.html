How to Play

Find all the theme words to fill the board.

  • Theme words match the theme described at the top.
  • Drag to connect adjacent letters (including diagonals).
  • Words must be at least 4 letters long.
  • The spangram describes the theme and spans two opposite sides of the board. It is highlighted in yellow.
  • Theme words are highlighted in blue.
  • Finding 3 non-theme valid words earns a hint.
  • A hint reveals the letters of one theme word.
  • Every letter is used exactly once.
